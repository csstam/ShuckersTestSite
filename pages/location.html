Location

The cottages are located on Flacks Road; it is approximately 2.5hours driving from Hobart or Launceston.

From the cottages, it is a leisurely 10 minute drive from Coles Bay village and Freycinet National Park, and only 8 minutes drive to Friendly Beaches National Park.
