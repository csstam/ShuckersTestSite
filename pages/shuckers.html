Shucker's Cottage

Shucker's Cottage has two bedrooms and is ideal for 2 couples.

The master bedroom has a queen bed and a single sofa bed. The second bedroom has a double bed.

The lounge and dining area is a shared space with a double sofa bed. There is a fully equipped kitchen, bathroom, separate toilet, laundry & outdoor gas BBQ & outside dining area.
