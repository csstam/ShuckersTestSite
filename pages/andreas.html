Andrea's Cottage

Andrea's Cottage is a three bedroom cottage which sleeps up to nine persons in proper beds
Andrea's provides three bedrooms: the master bedroom contains a queen sized bed and a single. Another room provides a double bed and set of signle bunks and the third room twin single beds. If required, there is an additional foldaway single bed available.

Andrea's Cottage has a kitchen containing all required cooking implements, a combined lounge and dining area, bathroom, separate toilet, laundry & an outside covered BBQ area in a fenced backyard.
